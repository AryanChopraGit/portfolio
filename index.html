About Me

I'm a passionate and creative individual currently pursuing a degree in Commerce. Before diving into the world of user experience, I honed my visual skills and explored my creative side as a freelance graphic designer. Beyond design, I'm deeply interested in Indian philosophy. Studying its rich traditions allows me to cultivate a broader perspective and approach design challenges with empathy and a deeper understanding of human experience. I find balance and inspiration in various aspects of life. When I'm not immersed in design or exploring philosophy, I enjoy listening to music and engaging in a good game of chess. This combination of design knowledge, spiritual curiosity, and creative spark allows me to approach projects with a unique perspective and a passion for creating meaningful experiences.
